Instructions for using NewsFinder

  • Click on Add-Ons->NewsFinder->Launch
  • This will launch the add-on in the sidebar
  • By default it gives you the latest news
  • Type in 3-4 terms to find news
  • Insert relevant links by clicking on "Insert Link"
  • Click on the headline or the open icon to open the article directly
  • Don't forget to email us for questions and post a review.
